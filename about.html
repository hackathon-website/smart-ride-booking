About Us

Welcome to Smart Ride Booking for People with Disabilities — a platform built to create inclusive, reliable, and accessible transportation for everyone.

Our mission is to remove barriers and ensure that people with disabilities can move independently and confidently. We offer features like:

  • Accessible vehicle options
  • Supportive drivers trained for disability care
  • Simple interface with screen-reader compatibility
  • Ride history tracking and easy rebooking

We believe in equality, safety, and convenience. Whether it's a daily commute or a special trip, we’re here to help you get there comfortably.
